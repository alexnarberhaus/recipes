Pasta Pesto

Delicious pasta with a fresh pesto sauce!

Description

A simple and delicious pasta dish with a fresh pesto sauce made from basil, pine nuts, and parmesan cheese. This will take around 15 minutes to make, but it will be as good as an 1h long pasta recipe!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb pasta of your choice

Preparation Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients for the pesto sauce in a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  3. Drain the pasta and mix with the pesto sauce.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
