Pasta Salad

Delicious pasta salad with a variety of fresh vegetables!

Description

A refreshing and healthy pasta salad with a variety of fresh vegetables and a tangy vinaigrette dressing. This is a perfect dish for a light lunch or dinner!

The preparation time is around 20 minutes and requires no prior cooking experience. Feel free to customize or change some ingredients to fit your taste! Enjoy!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 lb pasta of your choice
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, and red onion.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
