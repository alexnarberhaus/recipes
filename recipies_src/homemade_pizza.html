Description

A delicious and easy to make homemade pizza with a variety of toppings. This is a perfect dish for a fun dinner or lunch!

The preparation time is around 30 minutes and requires no prior cooking experience. Feel free to customize or change some ingredients to fit your taste! Enjoy!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers

Preparation Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
